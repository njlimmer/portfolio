Nancy Limmer

I have been a teacher for 22 years. I spent 8 years in the classroom teaching English, but for the last 14 years, I have been a junior high librarian.

I became interested in coding after getting involved in the Code.org education push to teach computer science in public schools. I began a coding club to introduce the principals of computer science to my students.

I started teaching myself to code because I needed to provide more of a challenge to my students, but it quickly became a true interest of my own. I love discovering how coding and design work together to create the user experience.
